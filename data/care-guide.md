# Kuma — Cat Care Guide

## Feeding Routine

- **Food brand:** Purina One Indoor Advantage (dry kibble)
- **Amount:** 1/4 cup, twice per day (morning and evening)
- **Schedule:** Feed at 7:00 AM and 6:00 PM
- **Forbidden foods:** No milk, no onions, no garlic, no chocolate, no grapes or raisins, no raw eggs, no dog food
- **Treats:** Up to 2 small treats per day; prefer freeze-dried chicken

## Litter Box

- **Box type:** Open top, large size
- **Litter type:** Clumping unscented clay litter
- **Scooping frequency:** Scoop once daily
- **Full change:** Replace all litter and wash the box every 2 weeks
- **Video reference:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=dQw4w9WgXcQ

## Medication

- Kuma takes no daily medication
- If a hairball appears, offer a small amount of hairball paste (kept in the kitchen cabinet) once per week

## Behavioral Notes and Quirks

- Kuma is shy with strangers; allow him to approach you first
- He likes being scratched behind the ears but dislikes having his belly touched
- He will meow near the door when he wants attention or play
- Favorite toy: feather wand — play for 10 minutes each evening to keep him calm at night
- If he hides under the bed for long periods, leave him alone; he will come out when comfortable
